ABOUT US


Half of the world’s population have limited access to healthcare — but help may be on the way in the form of medical delivery drones.


So we at DocDrone deliver the required medicines and healthcare products to remote areas using drones. We believe that medicine availability and delivery in these areas should be as smooth as those in big states and cities. We deliver the required goods overcoming the access barriers and enabling faster delivery to life saving supplies. Drones are useful in places where road connectivity and refrigerated transport are a challenge. To make the delivery faster and efficient we have setup our warehouses nearby the remote areas. So India "DOCDRONE" is at your service with lightning speed delivery.

Know About the Types of Drones


Fixed Hybrid

It can endure Long distance flight. It is the most used drone for delivery.


Single Rotor Drone

It has Long endurance (with gas power) and has Heavier payload capability.


Multi Rotor Drone

It can operate in a confined area. It has Short flight times and has Small payload capacity.
